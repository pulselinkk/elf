Local context

Lewisville is diverse.

Lewisville has more housing variety than most DFW cities — Old Town homes from the 1900s, mid-century from the 1960s-70s, established 1980s-90s neighborhoods, 2000s suburban growth, and modern luxury developments like Castle Hills and The Tribute (lakeside). This means we see every kind of project here.

Common Lewisville requests: (1) Updating dated 1980s-90s interiors — popcorn removal, cabinet painting, fixture upgrades. (2) Maintenance on Castle Hills luxury homes — premium paint, custom carpentry, high-end remodels. (3) Lakeside maintenance at The Tribute — exterior painting (lake humidity is brutal), deck staining, fence work.

Lake humidity factor

Homes near Lewisville Lake (especially The Tribute, Lake Vista Ranch, Lake Forest Park) deal with significantly higher humidity than inland DFW. This affects paint life (shorter), exterior wood (faster decay), and HVAC load. We spec exterior paint upgrades and use marine-grade caulk for lakeside homes.
