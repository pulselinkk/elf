Frequently asked questions.

Short answers for deciding whether this is DIY, maintenance, repair, or a professional project.

What is the first sign of bathroom water damage?

Swollen trim, soft flooring, stains, musty smells, or dark joints are common early signs.

Can I caulk over old caulk?

It is better to remove failed caulk, clean, dry, and apply new caulk correctly.

How long should the fan run?

A common habit is during the shower and at least 20 minutes after, but drying time matters most.

When is damage more than cosmetic?

Soft walls, loose tile, swollen subfloor, or active leaks mean deeper repair may be needed.
