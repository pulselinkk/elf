Local context

This is home.

ELF Home Services is based in Denton — we're Denton residents serving Denton homeowners. This means three things: (1) Fastest response in our entire coverage area — often within hours, not days. (2) Direct knowledge of every neighborhood, every era of construction, every common issue. (3) We're invested in this community — referrals from Denton neighbors are how this business grew, and we treat Denton work accordingly.

Denton's housing stock is wildly diverse for its size: 1880s-1930s historic homes around the Square and in Denton's historic districts, 1950s-1970s mid-century homes near UNT and TWU, 1980s-1990s established neighborhoods in west Denton, 2000s Robson Ranch and Wildridge, and 2010s+ The Trails of West Denton, Hidden Meadows.

UNT and TWU rental properties

Many Denton homes are rental properties for UNT and TWU students. We work with several Denton landlords on routine maintenance, tenant turnover repairs, and pre-listing fixes. Predictable per-task pricing, fast turnaround between tenants, and direct billing to property managers — call us about your portfolio.

Top services in Denton

What we get called for here.

Rental property maintenance — Denton has the highest student rental concentration of our coverage area. We do tenant turnover repairs (typically 4-12 hours of small fixes per unit), pre-listing prep, and ongoing maintenance for several Denton landlords.

Historic restoration — careful work on plaster walls, period trim, original wood floors. We don't pretend to be specialty restoration contractors, but we handle 90% of routine historic-home work with appropriate techniques.

Mid-range kitchen and bath remodels — Denton's housing prices mean most renovations are practical mid-range upgrades, not luxury custom builds. $15,000-$35,000 kitchen remodels are our typical Denton scope.

Same-day urgent work — being based in Denton means we can often respond to urgent calls within 2-3 hours. Active leak repairs, lockouts, security issues, etc.
