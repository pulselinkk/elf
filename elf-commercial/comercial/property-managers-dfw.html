FAQs

Property Managers questions.

Can you provide a COI?

ELF can support COI requests and vendor packet preparation. Any insurance wording, Additional Insured requirements, or endorsements should be confirmed against the active policy before work is promised.

Do you work with approval limits?

Yes. Property-management work can be routed around approval thresholds, owner sign-off, and tenant access rules.

Can you handle tenant turnover punch lists?

Yes. ELF can handle many practical make-ready and turnover items, including wall repair, paint, trim, doors, hardware, fixtures, caulk, tile, and closeout photos.
