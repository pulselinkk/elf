FAQs

Tenant Improvements questions.

Do you handle full tenant build-outs?

ELF handles practical finish-out, repair, punch, paint, fixture, and improvement scope. Regulated electrical, plumbing, fire, HVAC, structural, or permit-heavy work is routed to the proper specialist or contractor.

Can you work after hours?

Yes. After-hours and weekend scheduling is one of the main commercial advantages when a space needs work without disrupting operations.

Can you coordinate landlord documentation?

Yes. ELF can document work with photos, notes, invoices, COI support, and closeout summaries.
