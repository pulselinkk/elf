Frequently asked questions.

Short answers for deciding whether this is DIY, maintenance, repair, or a professional project.

What upgrade has the best ROI before selling?

Paint plus visible repairs often provides strong value because it changes buyer perception quickly.

Should I remodel the kitchen before selling?

Only if the current kitchen is a major obstacle and there is enough time and budget. Targeted refreshes are often safer.

Are bold colors bad for resale?

They can narrow buyer appeal. Neutral, warm, clean palettes usually photograph better.

Should I hide foundation cracks?

No. Repair cosmetic cracks appropriately, but serious movement signs should be evaluated honestly.
