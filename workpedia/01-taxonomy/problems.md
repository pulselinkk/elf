# Problem Taxonomy

## Visible Damage

| Problem | Likely Services | Quote Inputs | Hidden Risk |
|---|---|---|---|
| Peeling exterior paint | Painting, repairs | Photos, elevation, siding, age | UV, moisture, bad primer |
| Drywall crack | Drywall, painting | Crack length, location, seasonality | Foundation movement |
| Ceiling stain | Drywall, emergency, painting | Source, photos, softness | Active leak |
| Cracked grout | Tile, bathroom | Joint photos, wet/dry area | Movement or failed waterproofing |
| Warped flooring | Flooring, emergency | Material, moisture source | Slab moisture or plumbing leak |
| Sticking door | Repairs | Hinge side, latch side, frame gap | Foundation or humidity |
| Loose tile | Tile, bathroom | Hollow sound, substrate | Failed bond or water damage |
| Cabinet finish peeling | Painting, cabinets | Door material, finish, grease | Poor prep or thermofoil failure |
