What matters most in Plano.

Kitchen remodeling in Plano often starts with deciding whether the kitchen layout still serves the home. A simple finish update can be excellent when the work triangle, storage, and walkways are sound. But older galley and peninsula layouts sometimes deserve a deeper redesign because they block sight lines and make entertaining awkward.

ELF helps separate cosmetic updates from structural or trade-heavy changes. We look at cabinets, soffits, flooring continuity, electrical capacity, lighting, appliance locations, and whether the budget is better spent on refinishing or replacement.

Local FAQs.

Short answers to the questions homeowners usually ask before scheduling kitchen remodel work in Plano.

How much does a Plano kitchen remodel cost?

Plano kitchen projects vary widely. A finish-focused update may be in the $15,000 to $38,000 range, while deeper cabinet, layout, trade, and surface work can reach $75,000 or more depending on scope.

Can you remove kitchen soffits in Plano homes?

Sometimes. Soffits must be checked because they may hide ducts, wires, plumbing, or framing. The answer should be based on investigation, not assumption.

Is cabinet painting enough for an older Plano kitchen?

It can be enough if the layout works and the cabinet boxes are solid. If storage is poor, doors are failing, or appliance layout is awkward, replacement or modification may be the better investment.
