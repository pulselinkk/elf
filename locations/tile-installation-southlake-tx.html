What matters most in Southlake.

Tile in Southlake demands a higher level of pre-installation planning. Large-format tile magnifies low spots. Natural stone needs sealing and maintenance expectations. Custom showers require waterproofing, slope, drain planning, niche placement, and clean glass transitions. A backsplash with a premium range or hood needs centered layout and intentional cuts.

ELF plans tile installations as a complete surface system. We discuss tile size, substrate prep, waterproofing method, grout type, edge detail, pattern, movement joints, and how the tile meets cabinets, counters, glass, trim, or flooring.

Local FAQs.

Short answers to the questions homeowners usually ask before scheduling tile installation work in Southlake.

Why does large-format tile cost more to install?

Large-format tile is heavier, less forgiving, and requires flatter surfaces, careful handling, and more precise layout. The labor is different from standard tile installation.

Is porcelain or natural stone better for Southlake showers?

Porcelain is usually easier to maintain and highly durable. Natural stone can be beautiful but requires sealing and more careful cleaning. The best choice depends on desired look and maintenance tolerance.

Can you install a tile backsplash around existing counters?

Yes. The layout should account for counter levelness, outlet placement, cabinet lines, and focal points like ranges or hoods.
