Frequently asked questions.

Short answers for deciding whether this is DIY, maintenance, repair, or a professional project.

Is LVP good for bathrooms?

It can work in some bathrooms, but tile is usually better for wet, high-use spaces.

What tile size is best?

Large tile reduces grout lines but needs a flatter floor. Smaller tile can improve grip and slope control.

Are heated bathroom floors worth it?

They can be in premium remodels, especially when planned before tile installation.

What color is safest for resale?

Warm stone looks, soft gray-beige, and low-contrast patterns are usually safer.
