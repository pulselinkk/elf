LVP Flooring - Arlington, TX

Arlington LVP flooring install with slab prep, transitions, and a quieter finish.

An Arlington LVP flooring case study covering demo, slab prep, transitions, underlayment, plank direction, baseboards, and practical cost drivers.

CityArlington, TX
Timing2-5 day install window
Budget lensPer-square-foot flooring planning
The issue

What made this scope worth planning carefully.

LVP only looks premium when the floor underneath is ready. Older slab homes can hide dips, high spots, tack strip damage, door clearance issues, and transitions that need planning.

The plan

How ELF would sequence the work.

Remove old flooring cleanly, check the slab, solve obvious flatness problems, choose plank direction based on light and traffic, and decide whether baseboards are removed or shoe moulding is added.

The finish

The standard that makes the project feel complete.

A consistent plank direction, clean transitions, tight baseboard detail, and a durable wear layer make the floor feel like one continuous upgrade instead of room-by-room patches.

Scope checklist

  • Flooring demo and haul-off
  • Slab flatness review
  • LVP installation and stagger plan
  • Door jamb and transition detail
  • Baseboard or shoe moulding finish

Quote signals

  • Look for slab dips before ordering
  • Do not skip expansion gaps
  • Plan transitions at tile and carpet
  • Choose wear layer based on pets, kids, and rental use
