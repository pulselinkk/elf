How our pricing works

Three rules. That's it.

Rule 1

Free estimates always.

Visit your home, look at the work, give you a flat written price. No charge, no obligation, no high-pressure sales.

Rule 2

Flat rate over $200.

Any project over $200 is quoted at a fixed price. We absorb cost overruns from inaccurate estimates — that's our risk to manage, not yours.

Rule 3

Changes documented.

If we discover something that changes scope mid-project, we stop and quote the change before continuing. You always approve before we proceed.

A note on the lowest bid

If you receive a bid significantly lower than ours, ask three questions: (1) Are they licensed and insured (request COI)? (2) Do they offer a written labor warranty? (3) What materials are they using? Most "too good to be true" bids are missing one of those — and the savings disappear when the work fails or you're stuck with cleanup. Lowest is rarely cheapest in this trade.
