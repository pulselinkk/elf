Denton flooring needs to be chosen for the actual household. A rental near UNT may need scratch-resistant LVP, fast turn time, and simple transitions. Local FAQs.

Short answers to the questions homeowners usually ask before scheduling flooring installation work in Denton.

What flooring works best for Denton rentals?

A quality LVP is often the best balance of durability, water resistance, repairability, and cost. The exact product should be chosen for wear layer, texture, lock quality, and availability.

Do you need to level the slab before LVP?

Sometimes. LVP needs a reasonably flat substrate. Low spots, ridges, and old adhesive can show through or stress locking joints, so prep should be decided after the old flooring is visible.

Can flooring be installed while we live in the home?

Yes. Occupied flooring projects need staging, dust control, furniture plans, and clear room order. It is slower than an empty-house install but very manageable.
