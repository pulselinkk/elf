# DFW Location Signals - Volume 01

## Denton

Signals:

- Home base.
- Historic homes near the Square.
- UNT and TWU rentals.
- Robson Ranch low-disruption and low-VOC preference.
- Rayzor Ranch and newer construction.

Useful angles:

- Same-day response.
- Older-home repairs.
- Rental turns.
- Historic trim, paint, drywall, and careful prep.

## Frisco

Signals:

- Master-planned communities.
- 2000s and 2010s builder-grade upgrades.
- Open-plan kitchens.
- LVP replacing carpet.
- Cabinet refinishing instead of replacement.
- HOA-visible exterior standards.

Useful angles:

- Resale polish.
- Premium but practical remodels.
- Builder-grade to custom-feeling updates.
