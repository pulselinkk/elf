FAQs

Restaurants questions.

Do you work overnight?

Yes, when scheduled. Many restaurant repairs are best handled after closing or before service starts.

Do you handle kitchen equipment?

ELF can help with surrounding repairs, mounting, fixtures, doors, walls, and finishes. Specialized kitchen equipment, gas, electrical, plumbing, fire, and hood systems should go to the proper licensed specialist.

Can you help before inspection or reopening?

Yes. ELF can help clear visible punch items and flag specialist-first issues before a final walkthrough.
