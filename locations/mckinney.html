Local context

McKinney is two cities.

McKinney is unique in DFW — it's both one of the oldest cities (founded 1849, with significant Victorian-era housing) and one of the fastest-growing (population doubled since 2010). What you can do in a 1900-built downtown home is very different from what makes sense in a 2022 Trinity Falls house.

For historic homes (downtown McKinney, around the square): original lath-and-plaster walls (not modern drywall), older wood floors that deserve refinishing not replacement, decorative trim worth preserving. We approach these with restraint — preserve what's character, modernize what's failing. Common requests: structural drywall repair, careful exterior painting, period-appropriate trim work.

For newer McKinney (Stonebridge Ranch, Adriatica, Craig Ranch, Trinity Falls): standard modern construction. Most calls are upgrades from builder-grade to higher-spec materials.

Historic McKinney rule

If your home is in McKinney's historic district (around the courthouse square), exterior changes typically require Historic Preservation Officer approval. Color, materials, and design must match period appropriateness. We're familiar with the approval process and can help guide submissions.

Top services in McKinney

Skill where it counts.

Lath-and-plaster repair — historic McKinney homes have original plaster walls, not modern drywall. Modern joint compound doesn't bond well to old plaster. We use specialized plaster repair compound (Big Wally's, USG Plaster Bonder) and proper feathering technique to maintain the original character.

Tile retiling in older bathrooms — many 1990s-2000s McKinney master bathrooms (Stonebridge Ranch is full of these) need full retiles. We've done dozens with KERDI waterproofing for long-term durability against DFW hard water.

Exterior painting — McKinney's mix of brick, HardiePlank, and stucco needs different prep approaches per surface. Older downtown homes with painted brick need specialty masonry primers; HardiePlank in Stonebridge Ranch needs flexible exterior paint.

Period trim work — replicating original Victorian-era trim profiles for historic home repairs. We can match existing trim or work with custom millwork shops in the area.
