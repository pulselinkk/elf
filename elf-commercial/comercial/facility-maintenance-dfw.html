FAQs

Facility questions.

Do you handle preventive maintenance programs?

Yes. ELF can create recurring route visits for commercial properties, including inspection, repair, documentation, and recommended next steps.

Can you maintain several locations?

Yes. Multi-site routing is one of the main reasons commercial clients use ELF. Work can be grouped by city, priority, and access window.

What work should go to a specialist first?

Active plumbing, gas, electrical panel work, fire systems, roofing, structural issues, and HVAC refrigerant work should be routed to the proper licensed specialist. ELF flags those early.
