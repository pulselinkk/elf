Planner questions

Before you use the number.

Is this range a contractor quote?

No. It is an early planning range based on your selections. A written quote requires a review of dimensions, existing conditions, access, materials, and final scope.

Do I need to enter contact information?

No. The planner works without an account or contact details. Your draft remains in this browser unless you choose to share it.

What can change the final price?

Layout changes, concealed damage, plumbing or electrical relocation, structural work, permits, material selection, site access, and finish complexity can all change the price.

Can I send my plan to ELF?

Yes. The final screen can copy the brief, open a prefilled WhatsApp message, or transfer the choices into the quote request form.
