Local context

A premium community.

Flower Mound has been one of DFW's premium master-planned communities since the 1990s, and most of its housing stock reflects that — larger homes (typically 3,000-5,000 sqft), upper-mid to luxury pricing, established HOAs that maintain high standards. The result: most of our Flower Mound calls are higher-end work where quality matters more than lowest bid.

Common Flower Mound requests: (1) Premium kitchen remodels — semi-custom or full custom cabinets, quartzite or premium quartz, paneled refrigerators. (2) Master bathroom updates — typically curbless walk-in showers with linear drains, freestanding tubs, large-format porcelain. (3) Exterior maintenance — Flower Mound HOAs require well-maintained exteriors.

Flower Mound HOA reality

Most Flower Mound HOAs (Wellington, Bridlewood, The Trails) actively enforce exterior maintenance standards. If your fence is failing, your paint is fading, or your exterior trim is rotting, you'll get an HOA letter eventually. We work proactively with several Flower Mound homeowners on regular maintenance to stay ahead of HOA notices.
