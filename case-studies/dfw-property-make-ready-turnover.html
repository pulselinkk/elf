Property Pros - Dallas-Fort Worth

DFW make-ready turnover plan for photos, punch lists, and faster re-listing.

A DFW property make-ready case study for property managers, investors, and agents who need documented repairs before listing or tenant move-in.

CityDallas-Fort Worth
Timing1-5 day turnover window
Budget lensInvestor and manager planning
The issue

What made this scope worth planning carefully.

Make-ready work fails when everything is treated equally. The goal is not perfection; it is the right repair at the right visibility level before photos, showings, or tenant handoff.

The plan

How ELF would sequence the work.

Separate safety, function, photo-visible finish, and optional upgrades. Repair the items that block occupancy or presentation first, then document what remains.

The finish

The standard that makes the project feel complete.

Clean paint touch-ups, working doors and fixtures, crisp caulk, patched drywall, and photo documentation make the property easier to lease, sell, or hand back.

Scope checklist

  • Photo intake and triage
  • Safety and function repairs
  • Paint, drywall, caulk, and hardware
  • Listing-photo readiness
  • Closeout documentation

Quote signals

  • Prioritize photo-visible defects
  • Separate tenant damage from upgrades
  • Document all completed repairs
  • Schedule vendors before listing photos
Outcome

Managers can move faster because the repair list becomes a prioritized route instead of a loose collection of complaints.
