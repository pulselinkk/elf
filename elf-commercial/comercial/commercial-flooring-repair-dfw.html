FAQs

Flooring questions.

Do you replace entire floors?

ELF is best for focused commercial flooring repair, punch, transitions, tile, grout, baseboard, and small-area support. Large full-floor replacement may require a specialized flooring contractor.

Can you repair flooring after hours?

Yes. After-hours scheduling is often best when the affected area blocks customers, employees, or deliveries.

Can you help with trip hazards?

Yes. ELF can triage and stabilize many visible trip risks, then plan permanent repair or recommend a specialist when required.
