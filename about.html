The story

Why this
business exists.

ELF Home Services was founded after watching too many DFW homeowners get burned by the same broken contractor experience — overcharged, under-delivered, ghosted halfway through the work. The pattern was so consistent it felt structural.

We built ELF as the inverse of that experience. One team you talk to. Flat upfront pricing. The job done when promised. A real warranty in writing.

140+ projects later, we're proud that 94% of our customers come back or refer someone within a year. That's the only number that matters in this business.

Principles

How we work.

Five non-negotiables that govern every interaction. They aren't marketing — they're how we've built a referral-first business in DFW.

One point of contact — always.

You speak with our team from the first call to the final walkthrough. No dispatchers, no subcontractor handoffs, no "I'll have to check with my guy." Direct accountability, every step.

Flat upfront pricing — no surprises.

Quoted in writing before work begins. What you agree to is what you pay. If we discover something during the work that changes scope, we stop, show you, and quote the change before continuing — no end-of-job sticker shock.

1-year written labor guarantee.

Every job is backed by a one-year workmanship guarantee in writing. If anything we installed or repaired fails due to our work, we come back and fix it. No fine print. No arguments.

We start on time and we finish on time.

If we say we'll be there at 8 AM, we're there at 7:55. If a job takes 5 days, you get a 5-day timeline. Schedules slip in this industry constantly — we treat your time as seriously as our own.

Cleaned up daily.

Drop cloths down, tools off the floor at end of day, vacuumed before we leave. Your home should feel less like a construction site and more like a home you happen to be improving.

Credentials

Licensed. Insured. Accountable.

The basics every contractor should have but many don't bother with. We're documented and proud of it.

Insurance

General Liability

Full general liability insurance covering property damage and accidents during work. Certificate available upon request before any job starts.

Workers comp

Workers' Compensation

Workers' compensation coverage for any work performed on your property. Protects you from liability if anyone is injured on the job.

Trade partners

TSBPE & TDLR Pros

For licensed trade work (gas, electrical panel, refrigerant), we partner with vetted Texas-licensed plumbers (TSBPE) and electricians (TDLR). Code compliance guaranteed.
