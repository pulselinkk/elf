Frequently asked questions.

Short answers for deciding whether this is DIY, maintenance, repair, or a professional project.

What bathroom feature matters most to Southlake buyers?

A well-designed primary shower, strong vanity storage, and good lighting are usually more valuable than novelty features.

Are bold bathroom colors risky?

They can be. Soft, warm neutrals usually have broader resale appeal.

Is a wet room a good idea?

It can be, but only with excellent drainage, waterproofing, and layout.

Should I remove the tub?

If there is another tub in the home, removing a rarely used primary tub may be reasonable.
