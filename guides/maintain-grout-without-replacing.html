Homeowner checklist.

Use this before starting the project or asking for an estimate.

Checklist
  • Do not use acidic cleaners on natural stone.
  • Replace cracked caulk fully.
  • Dry showers faster with fan and squeegee.
  • Seal only clean, sound grout.
  • Stop if tile is loose.

Frequently asked questions.

Short answers for deciding whether this is DIY, maintenance, repair, or a professional project.

Can old grout be cleaned instead of replaced?

Yes if it is intact and only stained or dirty.

Does sealing grout fix stains?

Sealer helps resist future staining but does not repair damaged or dirty grout.

Why do shower corners crack?

Corners move. Flexible caulk is often appropriate at changes of plane.

When should grout be replaced?

When it is missing, crumbling, cracked, or no longer protecting the joint.
