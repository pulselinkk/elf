Local FAQs.

Short answers to common questions before scheduling cabinet refinishing work in Plano.

Is cabinet refinishing cheaper than replacement?

Usually yes when the cabinet layout and boxes are good. Replacement is better when function or structure is failing.

Will painted cabinets chip?

Any finish can chip under abuse, but proper cleaning, sanding, primer, coating, and cure time improve durability significantly.

What colors are safest for Plano resale?

Warm whites, soft taupes, balanced greige, and muted greens or blues tend to be safer than extreme trends.
