  • Decide if the range wall is the focal point.
  • Keep grout contrast low for resale.

Estimate tip

Decision rule

Protect the expensive layer.

When water, substrate, structure, or finish quality is involved, solve that layer before spending on visible finishes.

Frequently asked questions.

Short answers for deciding whether this is DIY, maintenance, repair, or a professional project.

What backsplash is safest for resale?

Warm white, soft neutral, low-contrast texture, and classic proportions are usually safest.

Is slab backsplash worth it?

It can be in premium kitchens, especially when easy cleaning and a quiet luxury look matter.

Should grout match the tile?

Often yes for open kitchens. High-contrast grout can make the wall busy.

Can backsplash be installed without changing counters?

Yes, if the existing counters are staying and the wall is sound.
