Local FAQs.

Short answers to common questions before scheduling painting work in Plano.

How much does painting cost in Plano?

Small room projects may start under $1,000, while full interiors, trim, cabinets, or exterior work vary by prep, height, surface condition, and materials.

Can old trim be painted?

Yes, if cleaned, sanded, primed, and coated correctly. Glossy or stained trim needs proper bonding prep.

Is one coat enough?

Rarely for a durable professional result. Coverage depends on color change, paint quality, sheen, and surface condition.
