How do property manager work orders start?

Send the property address, access notes, photos, tenant contact rules, approval limit, and urgency. ELF turns that into a scoped visit or estimate.

Can you bundle multiple work orders?

Yes. Bundling repairs by area or route can reduce mobilization and keep multiple units moving.

Do you handle occupied units?

Yes, with clear access windows and communication rules. Emergency risks are triaged first.

Can you help with owner reporting?

Yes. Photo documentation and itemized invoices are built for owner statements and dispute files.
