Local FAQs.

Short answers to the questions homeowners usually ask before scheduling hardwood flooring work in Plano.

Is engineered hardwood better than solid hardwood in Plano?

On many Plano slab foundations, engineered hardwood is the more practical wood option because it is more dimensionally stable than solid wood. The best choice still depends on moisture, installation method, and product quality.

Can existing hardwood be refinished instead of replaced?

Often yes, if it has enough wear layer and the damage is not too deep. Refinishing can be a strong choice when the existing floor has character and the layout still works.

What causes hardwood projects to cost more?

Subfloor prep, moisture mitigation, stair work, demolition, baseboard work, custom transitions, board repairs, and premium finishes are the usual cost drivers.
