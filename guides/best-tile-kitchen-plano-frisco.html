Frequently asked questions.

Short answers for deciding whether this is DIY, maintenance, repair, or a professional project.

What kitchen backsplash is safest for resale?

Soft neutral tile, handmade texture, classic proportion, and low-contrast grout are usually safe.

Is porcelain good for kitchen floors?

Yes. It is durable and water-resistant, but it needs proper substrate prep.

Should tile match the countertops?

It should coordinate, not duplicate. Too much pattern can feel busy.

Are dark grout lines a good idea?

They can be practical but visually strong. In open kitchens, low contrast is often better.
