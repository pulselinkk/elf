Local FAQs.

Short answers to common questions before scheduling flooring installation work in McKinney.

What flooring is best for McKinney homes?

LVP is practical for many active homes, while engineered hardwood can work well when moisture and flatness are addressed.

Can flooring be installed over tile?

Sometimes, but height, adhesion, transitions, and tile condition must be checked.

How long does flooring installation take?

Small areas can be quick; whole floors take longer because demo, prep, furniture, trim, and transitions control the schedule.
