# Service Taxonomy

## Core Services

| Service | Parent Category | Common Intent | Main Risk | Best Tool |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Painting | Surface finish | Refresh, resale, protection | Bad prep, wrong sheen, UV failure | Finish selector |
| Drywall | Wall systems | Repair, hide damage, restore texture | Texture mismatch, unresolved water | Patch planner |
| Tile | Hard surfaces | Wet-area durability, backsplash, design | Bad substrate, failed waterproofing | Layout planner |
| Flooring | Floor systems | Replace carpet, update resale, durability | Subfloor flatness, bad transitions | Room estimator |
| Kitchen | Remodel | Storage, layout, resale, daily function | Bad sequencing, cabinet lead time | Scope planner |
| Bathroom | Remodel | Water control, comfort, accessibility | Waterproofing failure | Wet-area checklist |
| Repairs | Handyman | Fix defects, bundle punch lists | Under-scoped small work | Punch-list builder |
| Appliances | Functional systems | Diagnose, repair, replace decision | Repair cost exceeds value | Repair math |
| Emergency | Stabilization | Stop damage, secure home | Hidden damage, unsafe conditions | Triage tool |
| Maintenance | Prevention | Avoid larger repairs | Deferred inspection | Seasonal checklist |
