A bathroom vanity seems like a cabinet choice, but it affects sink position, mirror height, outlets, tile edges, storage, cleaning, and whether the room feels custom or cramped.

Vanities are often chosen late, but they should be planned early. The width, depth, sink type, counter overhang, faucet style, and outlet placement all need to work together.

In DFW bathrooms, hard water, family use, and ventilation make material durability just as important as the cabinet style.

Frequently asked questions.

Short answers for deciding whether this is DIY, maintenance, repair, or a professional project.

Are floating vanities practical?

Yes when wall support and plumbing are planned correctly. They are less forgiving as a quick swap.

Is a double vanity always better?

Not always. Counter space and storage can matter more than a second sink.

What vanity is best for resale?

A clean freestanding or built-in vanity with durable counter and storage is broadly appealing.

Can ELF replace a vanity?

Yes. We can help with removal, fit, faucet, caulk, wall repair, and finish details.
