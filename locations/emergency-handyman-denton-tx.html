What counts as an emergency

When to call us immediately.

We define an emergency as anything that threatens the security, safety, or immediate habitability of your home. Common Denton emergency calls:

Security: Front door lock failure, door frame kicked in, broken sliding door latch, garage door that won't close. Weather damage: Denton gets severe thunderstorms — fence panels blown down, roof flashing pulled loose, window screens torn out. Water intrusion: Active roof leak with interior damage, burst supply line (after you've shut off the main), broken window letting water in. Structural: Tree limb through a fence or deck, broken deck railing that's unsafe to leave, ceiling that's visibly sagging after a leak.

For emergencies involving active water flow, always shut off your home's main water valve first, then call us.

Denton's storm profile

Why Denton gets more emergencies.

Denton County averages 28 tornado watches per year and sits in Tornado Alley's eastern edge. The combination of severe thunderstorms (hail, 60+ mph wind gusts), hot-dry-cold seasonal swings, and clay soil that moves seasonally creates more emergency home repair situations than most Texas cities. We keep materials stocked for the most common Denton emergency repairs: 1x6 cedar fence boards, temporary plywood for window boarding, door frame lumber and hardware, and EPDM roof tape for temporary leak stops.

After severe weather events, we typically have a 24-48 hour backlog of emergency calls. If it's a major storm, calling immediately gets you higher in the queue.

What we fix same-day

Our emergency repair capabilities.

Door and lock repairs: We carry common locksets, strike plates, and hinges. Most door failures are fixed in 1-2 hours. Fence panel replacement: We keep cedar boards in the truck. A 3-panel blown-down section is typically repaired the same day. Drywall emergency patching: When you have water intrusion, we can cut out wet drywall and install temporary sheeting to prevent further damage until a full repair can be scheduled. Window board-up: If a window is broken and can't be immediately replaced, we board it up securely with plywood and trim. Deck safety repairs: Broken railing, loose decking boards, or rotted support posts that are immediate safety hazards.
