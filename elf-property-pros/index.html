Questions

Property account FAQs.

Do you work with small landlords?

Yes. The program works for one rental, a small portfolio, or a larger management company. The account structure simply scales with volume.

Can you coordinate licensed trades?

Yes. ELF handles broad repair, make-ready, drywall, paint, flooring, tile, fixtures, and carpentry work. For licensed electrical, plumbing, gas, HVAC refrigerant, or specialist work, we coordinate the right licensed partner.

Do you provide photos?

Yes. Property work is documented with before and after photos and clear scope notes so owners, tenants, and agents can understand what was completed.

Can you invoice by property?

Yes. We can organize invoice notes by property, unit, work order, or owner reporting needs.
